Moonlight Association – Who are we?


In the Moonlight we've gathered a group of book lovers, passionate about their ability to touch souls. Because of that, we dedicate ourselves to promote human, familiar, spiritual and educational values through books, taking them in a critical and passionate way. In little words, we make reference to our motto: Thinking about reading, changing the world.

It's clear that reading is a form of entertainment, but we don't commune to it with the idea of a simple and hollow amusement. Nothing to far from reality, in fact, do you remember that made you look at the world with a different perspective?, or that book that inspired you to treat others more nicely?, or that another book where you found an irresistible character that made you think "I want to be like that"?.

Reading can create dreams and portray ideals, enriching the espectator and the society that goes through the pages. The crazy ones that conform Moonlight hang on to this cultural scene and guide ourselves with an idea, as simple as it is, has a big impact: We believe that the world can go in better ways, if us, the people who live in it change our hearts.

Our history


Moonlight was born in 2022 when a group of reading and writing amateurs decided to share what they know to the world.

Currently, our president is Patricia Tamayo, writer, reader and programming student; and our vice president is Rechel Medina, who is a programming and music student.

The directors of the reading and writing departments in America are Valeria Sanchez and Joselyn Navarro.

The Moonlight project has been growing thanks to the selfless collaboration of many people, with their voluntary dedication of time and professionalism, or with economical support.

To all of them, we want to express our most sincere gratitude, just like all of our subscribers, and to you, who is reading this lines in our website. All of you are, equally, Moonlight.
