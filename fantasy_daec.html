America's top teen fantasy book of the year. Start the new saga of the best-selling author around the world, Holly Black. Jude was seven years old when her parents were murdered and, along with her two sisters, she was transferred to the treacherous High Court of the Faerie Kingdom. Ten years later, all Jude wants, despite being a mere mortal, is to feel like she belongs here. But many of the inhabitants despise humans. She mind Prince Cardan, the youngest and wicked son of the High King. To gain a foothold in the Court, Jude must face him. And face the consequences. As a result, she finds herself embroiled in the intrigues and deceptions of the palace, as well as discovering her own knack for bloodshed. As civil war threatens to engulf the Faerie Courts, Jude is forced to risk her own life with a dangerous alliance.





Two wizards pitted against each other by fate compete in a deadly challenge in The Night Circus, the hypnotic bestseller that has captured the imagination of the world.The circus arrives without warning. It is not preceded by any advertisement. Suddenly it is there, where until the day before there was nothing. Inside the black and white striped tents, unique and dazzling events take place. It's called Le Cirque des Rêves, the circus of dreams, and it's only open at night.But, behind the scenes, a fierce rivalry develops: a duel between two young magicians, Celia and Marco, who have been trained since childhood by their instructors for this purpose. Although they do not know it, this is a game where only one can survive. Despite the enormous risks, Celia and Marco soon fall in love and unleash a series of fearsome consequences, which, with their ripple effect, leave the lives of everyone in the circus, from the performers to their owners, hanging in the balance.





Scarlett has never left the tiny island of Trisda, where she dreams of the wonders of Caraval, an annual week-long celebration in which the public participates in the spectacle.Caraval is magic. Mystery. Adventure. And for Scarlett and her beloved sister Tella, it represents freedom and a way to escape from her ruthless father.When the sisters finally receive their long-awaited invitations to Caraval, it seems that their dreams have come true. But Tella disappears moments after her arrival, kidnapped by the show's organizer, Legend.Scarlett understands that everything that happens in Caraval is a complex illusion. However, she is soon plunged into a dangerous game full of love, magic and heartbreak. Real or not, she must find Tella before she ends the game or she will lose her sister forever.




Harry Potter has never heard of Hogwarts when yellowish parchment envelopes, addressed in green ink and sealed with purple wax, begin to rain down on the doormat at 4 Privet Drive. And though Harry's horrible uncles are quick to confiscate the letters, on Harry's eleventh birthday a gigantic man named Rubeus Hagrid, whose eyes glow like black beetles, bursts in with extraordinary news: Harry Potter is a wizard, and he has been granted a place at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. An amazing adventure is about to begin.
